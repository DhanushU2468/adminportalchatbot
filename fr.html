About Us

Welcome to the SJC Institute of Technology, a center of excellence in technical education. Established in 1997, we are committed to fostering innovation, research, and academic excellence in engineering and technology fields.

Our state-of-the-art campus is equipped with modern laboratories, workshops, and resources to nurture the growth of future technologists and entrepreneurs.

Departments

  • Department of Computer Science - Focusing on software development, data analysis, and AI research.
  • Department of Electrical Engineering - Specializing in electrical circuits, power systems, and renewable energy solutions.
  • Department of Mechanical Engineering - Offering hands-on experience in mechanical systems, robotics, and manufacturing.
  • Department of Civil Engineering - Providing in-depth knowledge on construction, structural analysis, and infrastructure development.
  • Department of Management Studies - Preparing students for leadership roles with courses in business management and entrepreneurship.
